It Began In The Schau Studio

GMH pedals was founded in Vancouver B.C. by Grave Disorder Bass Guitarist Garth MacGregor Heslop and developed from input from a collaboration of journeyman guitarists that wanted to turn the thin, clean sound of their electrics into something wild and distorted. Garth manufactured his first protoype in his Yaletown condo with great demand from the local music scene and GMH pedals was born

The Crunch!

Transparent Overdrive

The GMH Pedal Crunch Transparent Overdrive is a low-gain overdrive pedal suitable for guitar and bass; it provides soft, tube-like clipping while maintaining your core tone. Gain: This controls the amount of amplification in both gain stages.
Tone: A wide range control that works very much like the tone pot on most electric guitars.
Vol: This attenuates the amplified and clipped signal after it leaves the tone filter.
LED Brightness As a player who occasionally plays daytime outdoor shows, I wanted a super bright LED; however, this made it horrible to use in any other situation. So, I added a variable resistor (trim pot) to the side that allows you to vary the LED Brightness from dark stage to bright sun. Please make the adjustment gently using a jeweler’s screwdriver.
