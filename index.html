About GMH Crunch Pedals

Established in 2018

It Began In The Schau Studio

GMP pedals was founded in Vancouver B.C. by Grave Disorder Bass Guitarist Garth MacGregor Heslop and developed from input from a collaboration of journeyman guitarists that wanted to turn the thin, clean sound of their electrics into something wild and distorted. Garth manufactured his first protoype in his Yaletown condo with great demand from the local music scene and GMH pedals was born
